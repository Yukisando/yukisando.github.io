A little about me



As a gaming enthusiast and musician, I oriented my studies to become a sound engineer and an avid coder. Proficient in C# (Unity) with a C++ background, I focus on building performant readable code while delivering passionate work. Building applications from scratch forced me to work with various languages and technologies and grasp a broader understanding of what it takes to finish a product.

Outside of game-making, my hobbies in dev revolve around creating plugins, expanding my music portfolio and keeping up with the latest in hardware and software technologies.

In the last couple of years, my focus has been on gamification and more specifically serious games. I got to study and work in numerous countries across the world, giving me the opportunity to meet a great number of professors and colleagues with vastly different backgrounds and cultures.
This in turn allowed me to improve my communication skills and develop an open mindset, both I believe, crucial in conducting effective research and meeting client needs.
