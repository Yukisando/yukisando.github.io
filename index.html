A little about me



As a gaming enthusiast and musician, I oriented my studies to become a sound engineer as well as an avid coder. Being proficient in C# (Unity), Java, and with strong C/C++ knowledge, I focus on building performant readable code while delivering passionate work.

Although most of my time is spent making games and applications on Unity, I also love creating plugins for games like Minecraft or World of Warcraft and composing music and sound effects for my apps and music portfolio.

I got to study and work all over the world; From Canada to Singapore and Australia as well as most of Europe which greatly improved my communication skills and helped me develop an open mindset and an international orientation.
