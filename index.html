A little about me



As an enthusiast gamer and a musician, I oriented my studies to become a sound engineer as well as an avid coder. Being proficient in C++, Java, Lua and Unity(C#), I believe I now have the tools to deliver substantial passionate work.

Most of my free time is spent making games, creating plugins for Minecraft, World of Warcraft and making VSTs and compositions on MAX MSP and Ableton Live.

I traveled a lot during my youth, having lived in Canada, Singapore, the Netherlands, France, Belgium, Switzerland and more recently the UK and Australia. Theses several movings greatly improved my communication skills and my open-mindedness. Working with classmates and professors from all around the world provided me with a truly international background.
