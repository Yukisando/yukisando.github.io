A little about me

My dream job, for as long as I can remember, has been to work in the video game industry. As an enthusiast gamer and a musician, I oriented my studies towards becoming a sound engineer as well as an avid coder.

Having learned C++, Java, Lua, Unity C# and some more, I feel I now have the tools to get closer to my ultimate goal.

Most of my free time is spent making games, creating plugins for Minecraft, World of Warcraft and making VSTs and compositions using MAX MSP and Ableton Live.

I traveled a lot during my youth, having lived in Canada, Singapore, the Netherlands, France, Belgium, Switzerland and more recently the UK and Australia.

Shooter Hero

Shooter Hero is a 2D shooter game made entirely on C++ using OF library. Part of a second year project, I had to make a C++ application using inheritance, polymorphism, concurrency and smart pointers. Working on this game allowed me to better organize the hierarchy of my code and make sure it is running as fast and safely as possible.
